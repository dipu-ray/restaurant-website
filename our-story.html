Our Story

Since 1997

A Kitchen Built on Conviction

Ember & Oak was born in a converted 1920s warehouse in the city's meatpacking district. Founder and Executive Chef Marcus Calloway had a single ambition: to make the wood fire the centrepiece of every meal.

"Fire is honest. It doesn't lie. It tells you exactly how ready the meat is, how hot the pan is, how much attention the cook is paying."

Twenty-seven years later, that same fire burns at the heart of our kitchen. We've earned four Michelin stars, been named "Best Steakhouse in America" three times, and served tens of thousands of guests — yet our philosophy has never changed.

Marcus Calloway

Founder & Executive Chef
