The Experience

Beyond the Ordinary Meal

At Ember & Oak, dining is a ritual. From the first amuse-bouche to the final nightcap, every detail is considered, every ingredient sourced with intention.

🔥

Wood-Fire Technique

Our custom-built wood-fire grill reaches 1,400°F — the only way to achieve that perfect char while preserving the depth of flavour within.

🍷

Sommelier-Led Wine Program

Over 180 labels curated by our in-house sommelier. Complimentary pairing consultations available for every table.

🌿

Farm-to-Table Sourcing

We partner with 12 regional farms and fisheries, rotating the menu seasonally to honour the ingredients at their peak.

🎩

Private Dining Suites

Three private rooms for celebrations, business dinners, and milestone moments — each with a dedicated chef and service team.
